Frequently Asked Questions
Q: How can I book movie tickets online?

A: To book movie tickets online, follow these steps:

  1. Visit our website or mobile app.
  2. Select your preferred movie and showtime.
  3. Choose your seats and the number of tickets.
  4. Proceed to payment and complete the transaction.
  5. Receive your confirmation and e-tickets via email or app.

Q: Can I cancel or refund my online movie ticket?

A: Policies vary, but generally, movie tickets purchased online are non-refundable. Please check the terms and conditions during the booking process.

Q: How do I access my e-tickets?

A: After completing the booking, you will receive an email or app notification with your e-tickets. Simply present the e-tickets at the theater's entrance for entry.
